About Us

Global Graduate Empowerement


Global Grad Empowerment was established in 2024 as an organisation that is led and owned by female, black professionals and entrepreneurs, who geared into being significant role players in the economic and commercial field within South Africa and its neighbouring states. The aim of starting Global Grad was to give back to the community through the creation of employment and opportunities. By merging the gap when it comes to the empowerment of local communities through education, youth development.


The organisation was founded by Ms Molatelo Maila whose passion is to help vulnerable unemployed youth with qualifications and by equipping them with tools and resources which will make a positive change in their lives. Ms Maila’s ideas came through her exposure from her working environment within an education sector where she gained the necessary knowledge, skills and experience to achieve goals and objectives. Ms Maila is a BA Law graduate from University of the Witwatersrand with experience and exposure of being a student leader within residences and has worked within the Central Residence Space.

Mission & Vision

A national database of youth skills and qualifications

Promote global youth collaboration

Equal access to educational exchange programmes

Access to leadership programs

Foster the employability of young graduates

Objectives

  • Establish our existence and goals through youth platforms (Social media)
  • Facilitate on-demand skills development and computer literacy programs
  • Facilitate educational webinars and networking events
  • Provide support for global educational/employment applications
  • Increase accessibility for educational and leadership tours globally
  • Foster youth cooperation between the BRICS countries
